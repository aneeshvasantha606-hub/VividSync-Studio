POOJA P

Role: Fashion Designer | Creative Stylist | Trend Visionary

"Fashion isn’t just about fabric—it’s about feeling empowered in your own art."

Pooja is a designer who blends culture, technology, and artistry to redefine modern style. Her work celebrates individuality—turning threads into statements of identity.

At VividSync Studio, she leads conceptual design for fashion visuals, fabric storyboards, and aesthetic branding. Her vision revolves around sustainability, structure, and storytelling, ensuring every piece she designs carries a message of confidence and creativity.

Signature Collections & Showcases

  • Urban Bloom 2025: A sustainable fashion line fusing recycled textiles with futuristic design language.
  • “Threads of Tomorrow”: Digital fashion concept for AR-based virtual styling platforms.
  • Visual Wardrobe Campaign: Directed a lookbook for VividSync Studio’s design identity visuals.
  • Runway Harmony: Collaborated with architects and photographers to synchronize fashion with structure and light.

Design Philosophy

Pooja believes fashion is living art—a language without words. Her design approach merges minimalism with motion, where every curve and texture speaks of purpose.
